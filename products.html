Product Overview

Our portable device is designed with the latest AI technology to assist aphasia patients in their treatment and communication. It combines cutting-edge neuromorphic AI chips with a user-friendly interface to deliver exceptional performance and ease of use.

Key Features

  • Advanced neuromorphic AI chips for real-time processing
  • High-quality audio signal processing for clear communication
  • Embedded AI and LLM for personalized assistance
  • Lightweight and portable design
  • Long battery life for extended use
  • User-friendly interface for easy operation

Usage Guide

Our device is simple to use and comes with a comprehensive user manual. Here are the basic steps to get started:

  1. Charge the device using the provided charger.
  2. Power on the device by pressing the power button.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to set up your profile.
  4. Use the touch screen to navigate through the menu and select the desired function.
  5. For detailed instructions, refer to the user manual or watch our tutorial videos below.
